Get Involved

Together, We Can Make a Difference

At Bahay, we believe in the power of collective action to address homelessness in New Jersey. Your involvement, whether big or small, can create a positive impact on the lives of individuals and families facing homelessness. Here are several ways you can get involved:

Volunteer Your Time and Skills

Direct Service Volunteer

Join us in providing direct assistance at shelters. Whether it's serving meals, distributing clothing, or offering a friendly smile, your time makes a difference.

Skill-Based Volunteer

Contribute your unique skills. Whether you're an IT expert, counselor, or educator, your professional expertise can empower those in need.
