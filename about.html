About Bahay

Welcome to Bahay, where compassion meets action in the fight against homelessness.

What We Offer

Shelter Directory

Discover a comprehensive directory of shelters across New Jersey. Whether you're looking for a place to stay or want to volunteer your time, Bahay is your go-to resource.

Volunteer Opportunities

Bahay makes it easy for you to make a difference. Explore diverse volunteer opportunities at local shelters and organizations, and join us in creating positive change.

Our Mission

At Bahay, we believe in the power of community, kindness, and hope. Our mission is simple yet profound—to spread awareness about homelessness in New Jersey and provide practical solutions to address this pressing issue. We envision a world where every person has a safe and warm place to call home.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Bahay is more than a website; it's a community of compassionate individuals united in the pursuit of a world without homelessness. Join us on this journey, and together, let's make a lasting impact—one shelter, one volunteer, one act of kindness at a time.

Thank you for being a part of the Bahay family.

Together, we can build a brighter and more compassionate future.
