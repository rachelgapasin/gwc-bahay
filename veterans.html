New Jersey Homeless Veterans

We address a significant contributing factor to veterans' homelessness: a high prevalence of trauma, mental illness, and substance addiction, with growing challenges.
One in five of the soldiers who served in Iraq and Afghanistan have been diagnosed with mental health disorders, accounting for around half of the total.

Program and Relief Centers

National Call Center for Homeless Veterans

It's free and confidential. You'll get access to trained VA counselors. It's available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week You'll get information about VA homeless programs, health care and other services in your area
1-877-4AID VET (877-424-3838)

Hope For Veterans Programs

"Hope for Veterans Programs, we have rescued over 6,000 veterans and their families from homelessness and despair. We provide transitional housing, permanent supportive housing, rapid re-housing and homelessness prevention. The services we provide are essential to our veterans and include mental health counseling, transportation and employment counseling."

Veteran's Haven North

"Veteran's Haven North will ensure that homeless veterans are treated and cared for in a dignified, compassionate, and professional manner so as to reach an optimal level of independence and aid them in re-acquiring the life skills and ability to live in the community while maintaining their self-sufficiency, dignity, and honor."

Arborland Veteran Foundation

"Arborland Veteran Foundation uses a network of resources and the support of the community to provide crucial assistance to find New Jersey homeless veterans the help they need. We are dedicated to supplying valuable homeless veteran assistance New Jersey veterans rely on to keep going and live the kind of life they have earned by their service to our country."
